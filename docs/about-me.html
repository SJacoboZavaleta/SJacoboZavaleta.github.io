Sergio Jácobo is a passionate Mechatronics Engineer driven to design innovative clinical robots and biomedical devices. With expertise in artificial intelligence and machine learning, he develops cutting-edge solutions to improve healthcare. His academic research on robotics and medical imaging has been published in peer-reviewed journals.

Outside of work, Sergio applies his programming skills to build apps and explore new technologies. He also enjoys unwinding through films, books, and outdoor activities like hiking and cycling. With a lifelong curiosity for learning, he actively seeks new challenges that combine his interests in engineering, computer science, and helping others.
